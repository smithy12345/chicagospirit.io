Chicago Spirit Synchronized Skating Teams

Bringing elite synchronized skating teams to the South-West Chicago Suburbs.

Athlete State of Mind

Quality training focused on treating skaters like athletes. Skaters will get the training they need in multiple disciplines to be well rounded competitors on and off the ice.

Why Join?

Join an organization that is committed to making a pathway to national and international competitive teams. Join an organization that cares about growing your skater's potential. Chicago Spirit will be fielding qualifying level teams for the 2017-2018 season through sr. There is currently no senoir level team in the Chicago-land area. High level skaters have no opportunity to reach the senoir level. Representing one's country is the greatest experience synchronized skaters can have.

Chicago-lands newest synchronized skating team

Building an elite program right in your backyard. Synchronized skating is an ever changing, and adapting sport, be a part of an organization that welcomes the nwe synchro atmosphere. Have your skater move on to college teams prepared. Chicago Spirit is dedicated to creating an elite program for skaters looking to take the next step towards excellence,

Contact Us

Training Regiment

We are changing up how teams practice in the Chicago area. Each weekend practice is more than just on ice. Teams will have edge and power classes, figures, and even ice dance classes. As well as, teams will get a variety of off classes from ballet and stretch to character training and team building. This helps your skater be the best they can be. Other teams won't change the way they train, but we will.

IJS Preparation

Bringing in the top IJS technical help as well as choreographers, and pair specialists. Chicago Spirit strives to providing teams with programs that are competition ready and competition.

Fundraising

Fundraising where you see an immediate impact on your own bill. Fundraising that gives you a chance to see a visible reward back on the individual level, but also the team level.

A Family

Be a part of something more than yourself. Be a part of a family. Building more than just athletes, but well rounded individuals.
